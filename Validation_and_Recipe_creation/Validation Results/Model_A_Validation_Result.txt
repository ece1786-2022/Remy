RECIPE NAME
Tom & Jerry 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 ounce Jerry Ferrara rum
1/2 ounce Benedictine
1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
1/2 ounce simple syrup
1 egg white
Garnish: nutmeg, freshly grated
Garnish: green olives

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Add the rum, Benedictine, lemon juice and simple syrup into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
Strain into a rocks glass over crushed ice.
Top with the egg white and stir gently to combine.
Garnish with freshly grated nutmeg.

RECIPE NAME
Hot Toddy 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 ounces vodka
1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
1/2 ounce simple syrup
Garnish: lemon twist

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Add the vodka, lemon juice and simple syrup to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora glass.
Express the oils from a lemon twist over the drink, then discard.
