RECIPE NAME
Better Than Sex Cake 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 package cream cheese
4  eggs
1 cup evaporated milk
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
¼ cup milk
1 package powdered sugar
½ cup shortening
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ¼ cups white sugar

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Beat shortening, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a bowl using an electric mixer until smooth. Add confectioners' sugar; beat until smooth.
Combine cream cheese, evaporated milk, and sugar in a bowl; beat until smooth. Add powdered sugar and mix until smooth. Stir in pecans. Spread icing over cake.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes. Cool cake completely on wire rack.

RECIPE NAME
Golden Rum Cake 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ cup butter, softened
2  eggs
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ½ cups white sugar

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
Beat butter and sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until light and fluffy. The mixture should be noticeably lighter in color. Add eggs one at a time, allowing each egg to blend into the butter mixture before adding the next. Beat in vanilla extract and flour until smooth.
Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together in a medium bowl. Add flour mixture to butter mixture alternately with milk, beginning and ending with the flour mixture. Mix until just incorporated; do not overmix. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Cool cake completely on a wire rack before icing.

RECIPE NAME
Russian Honey Cake 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup butter
½ cup butter
½ cup chopped walnuts
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1  egg
1 cup milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ½ cups white sugar

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
Beat butter and white sugar together in a bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Add flour mixture alternately with milk, mixing just until incorporated. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool cake completely before frosting with your favorite frosting.

RECIPE NAME
Chocolate Chestnut Cake 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups buttermilk
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1 package cream cheese, softened
3  eggs
1 cup grated chocolate chips
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground cloves
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 teaspoon salt
1 ½ cups sifted confectioners' sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 10 inch tube pan. Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and confectioners' sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the buttermilk, mixing just until incorporated. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.
To Make Frosting: In a medium bowl, combine confectioners' sugar, cream cheese and 1 cup chopped walnuts. Beat until smooth. Spread on cooled cake.

RECIPE NAME
Jewish Coffee Cake 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking soda
¼ cup butter, softened
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup confectioners' sugar
1  egg
1 cup flaked coconut
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup white sugar

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 10x15 inch jellyroll pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar and baking soda until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg, then the flour, then the vanilla. Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the confectioners sugar. Stir in the coconut.
In a separate small bowl, combine the chopped walnuts and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir into the creamed mixture alternately with the melted butter. Spread batter evenly into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 60 to 70 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool.

RECIPE NAME
Ricotta Pie 

RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
½ cup butter, softened
1 cup chopped pecans
2  eggs
1 cup flaked coconut
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ¼ cups vegetable oil
2 cups white sugar

RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, blending well after each. Stir in coconut and vanilla.
Pour flour mixture into prepared pans. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool on wire racks for 10 minutes.
While cakes cool, make icing: Combine pecans, sugar, oil, eggs, vanilla, and flour in a large bowl. Beat until fluffy.
Spread icing over cooled cake layers. Refrigerate until set, about 1 hour.
