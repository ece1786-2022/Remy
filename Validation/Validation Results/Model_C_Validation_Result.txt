DRINK RECIPE NAME
Tom & Jerry

DRINK RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS

CAKE RECIPE NAME
Better Than Sex Cake 

CAKE RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ cup butter
½ cup butter, softened
1 cup chopped pecans
4  egg whites
4  egg yolks
3  egg yolks
2 cups flaked coconut
1 ½ cups packed brown sugar
¼ teaspoon salt
½ cup shortening
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
½ cup water
2 cups white sugar

CAKE RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
Beat white sugar, water, egg yolks, and shortening together in a bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Beat butter and white sugar mixture together in a separate bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in shortening mixture until just combined. Add flour mixture and vanilla extract; beat until just combined. Fold coconut and pecans into batter. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool on a wire rack.

CAKE RECIPE NAME
Golden Rum Cake 

CAKE RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ cup butter
½ cup butter, softened
1 cup chopped walnuts
4  eggs
1 cup flaked coconut
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 cups packed brown sugar
½ teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 cups white sugar

CAKE RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the batter alternately with the buttered coconut. Spread batter evenly into the prepared pan.
Bake for 55 to 60 minutes in the preheated oven, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely.

CAKE RECIPE NAME
Russian Honey Cake 

CAKE RECIPE INGREDIENTS
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
¼ cup butter, softened
1 cup buttermilk
1 cup chopped pecans
2  egg whites
2  egg yolks
1 ¼ cups flaked coconut
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup sour cream
1 ½ cups white sugar

CAKE RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
Beat sugar and flour together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until fluffy. Beat in sour cream and egg yolks until light and fluffy. Add flour alternately with buttermilk, mixing just until incorporated. Fold in pecans; mixing just enough to evenly combine. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour 20 minutes. Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Run a table knife around edge of pan and invert cake onto a serving plate. Serve warm.

1 teaspoon baking soda
½ cup butter
½ cup butter, softened

CAKE RECIPE NAME
Ricotta Pie 

CAKE RECIPE INGREDIENTS
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup butter, softened
3  eggs
4  eggs
1 ½ cups milk
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup shortening
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 ½ cups white sugar

CAKE RECIPE INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Beat shortening and sugar together in a bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, mixing until fully incorporated; stir in vanilla. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter mixture alternately with milk. Spread batter into the prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Run a table knife around the edges to loosen, then invert carefully onto a serving plate or cooling rack. Let cool completely.
